About me

Introduction

My name is Robert Mensing, and I am a Full Stack designer/developer based in Bryan, TX USA.

I have been coding, developing, designing, and tinkering for many years as self-taught hobbiest and currently transitioning into professional development.

I am an avid Maker with interests in microcontrollers and mini-PCs such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi, Home Automation, Home Theatre using Plex, Kodi and other software, and CAD / CAM / CNC machining.
